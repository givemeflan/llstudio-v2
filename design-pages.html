About the Project:

I call this project(s), a mini collection of design pages. One of my favorite things about being a Front End Web Developer, are the designs and layouts you can come up with. Even the simplest of designs, fonts, or colors can make a huge impact.

I displayed 4 of these pages just to show how photos, colors, and placement of text can make a difference and give off a different feel depending on what you are going for.

The first 2 pages were inspired by my clients who are small business owners. The last 2 were inspired by my love of space, NASA, and The Expanse,

What's Involved:

HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, VSC, Google Fonts, Unsplash, Color Palettes, fun, space, food
