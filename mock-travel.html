About the Project:

This project we are looking at is a 1 page mock city inspired website. Blue colored Dubai photos were my inspiration. I wanted to play around with some simple design schemes and colors.

​Java and Spring Boot were use to connect the Contact or Email Us form to a database. For now, the site is launched through Heroku.

What's Involved:

HTML5, CSS3, Bootsrap, Java, Spring Boot, VSC, Google Fonts, Pexels, Color Palettes, Font Awesome
